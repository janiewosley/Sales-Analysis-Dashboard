# 📊 Sales Overview Dashboard | Power BI

> An interactive Power BI dashboard that transforms sales data into actionable business insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, and regional performance analysis.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Sales Overview Dashboard** is a Business Intelligence project developed in **Power BI** to help stakeholders monitor sales performance across products, sales representatives, cities, and regions.

The dashboard provides a centralized view of key business metrics, enabling users to identify sales trends, compare regional performance, evaluate product profitability, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Many organizations rely on static spreadsheets that make it difficult to monitor sales performance and identify growth opportunities.

This dashboard addresses this challenge by providing an interactive reporting solution that allows users to:

- Track product profitability
- Monitor revenue generated by sales representatives
- Analyze customer activity over time
- Compare sales performance across Nigerian cities
- Filter reports dynamically for faster decision-making

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Modeling

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Product Performance
Displays the most profitable products, allowing management to identify best-selling items and optimize inventory planning.

### Sales Representative Performance
Compares revenue generated by each sales representative, making it easier to evaluate individual performance.

### Customer Trend Analysis
Shows customer activity by month to reveal seasonal purchasing patterns and customer engagement.

### Geographic Analysis
Visualizes sales distribution across Nigerian cities using an interactive map.

### Interactive Filters
Users can dynamically filter reports by:

- Product Category
- Region
- City

---

## 📊 Key Business Metrics

The dashboard enables users to analyze:

- Revenue by Sales Representative
- Product Profitability
- Customer Count
- Geographic Sales Distribution
- Monthly Customer Trends

---

## 💼 Business Value

This dashboard helps decision-makers to:

- Identify top-performing products
- Evaluate employee sales performance
- Understand customer purchasing trends
- Compare regional sales performance
- Make faster, data-driven business decisions

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

The dataset was cleaned and transformed using **Power Query** by:

- Correcting inconsistent data types
- Preparing datasets for analysis
- Creating relationships between tables

## 📐 Data Modeling

A relational data model was developed to support interactive reporting by connecting sales, products, customers, and geographic data.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Laptop A13 generated the highest profit among all products.
- Peter recorded the highest revenue among the sales representatives.
- Customer activity remained relatively consistent during the reporting period before declining sharply in May.
- Sales transactions were concentrated in major Nigerian cities including Lagos, Abuja, Kano, and Port Harcourt.
- Interactive filters enable users to drill down into specific product categories, regions, and cities for deeper analysis.
